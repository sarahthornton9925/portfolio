Hello! My name is Sarah Thornton.

I'm a junior at Northwestern University studying psychology and design, drawn to the intersection of those two interests.

My goal is to create thoughtful, human-centered designs that make people feel seen, understood, and genuinely comfortable in the spaces they move through.

This summer, I'll be working at Andalusia Design, a custom parametric design and architectural fabrication services startup in Austin, TX. I'm excited to add a whimsical touch to the things I create and the people around me.
